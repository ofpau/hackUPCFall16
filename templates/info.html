Information

Mobile Data Tracker shows on a map information collected by users' phones: where they were, and interesting information such as how intense their signal was or which carrier provided them connection. Each user is represented by a circle. There are many colors -each one represents a carrier. The more intense is the color, the more intense is their signal. Click on a circle and get clear-cut information as the exact coordinates. Inquiries with precise data as "users from a specific month/year and carrier" can be made. As a result interesting data for carrier companies can be concluded. When had they the most active users? Or, how many people active users have they on a given location? All the data is obtained from the vast csv files provided by the tech company Altran.
